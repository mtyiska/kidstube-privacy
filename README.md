# KidsTube Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 11, 2026**

KidsTube is a parent-controlled video app designed for children. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

- **Name and Email Address** — collected via Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple when a parent creates an account.
- **User ID** — a unique identifier assigned by Firebase to your account.
- **Watch History** — which videos were watched by child profiles and for how long. Used to show parents viewing activity.

## How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate your account and keep it secure
- To save your child profiles and approved video playlists
- To display watch history to parents in the dashboard

## We Do Not

- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Allow children to share personal information
- Track users across apps or websites
- Collect location data

## Children's Privacy

KidsTube is designed for use by children under parental supervision. Child profiles do not require any personal information. Parents control all content and account settings. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children.

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase (Google)** — used for authentication and data storage.
- **YouTube** — videos are played via the YouTube embedded player.
- **Google Sign-In** — optional authentication method.

## Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account exists. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
